What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. These files contain data that help websites remember your actions and preferences over time, so you don’t have to re-enter them whenever you return or browse from one page to another. Cookies also help website owners understand how visitors engage with their content.

How We Use Cookies

At ModMorrowind Store, we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and improve our services. Specifically, cookies help us to:

  • Remember your preferences, such as language settings or login details
  • Keep your shopping cart updated as you navigate our site
  • Analyze site traffic, measure performance, and identify ways to improve usability
  • Provide personalized recommendations and show products relevant to your interests

These cookies may be session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, or persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.

Third-Party Cookies

We may partner with trusted third-party service providers, such as analytics and advertising tools, who set their own cookies on your device. These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website and deliver targeted content. Please note, these third parties have their own privacy and cookies policies, which we recommend reviewing separately.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser’s settings. Most browsers allow you to view which cookies are stored on your device and delete them if desired. However, please be aware that disabling cookies might limit certain features and could negatively affect your shopping experience, such as keeping you logged in or remembering items in your cart.

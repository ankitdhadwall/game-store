What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help make websites work efficiently and provide information to site owners. Cookies can store user preferences, keep users logged in, and track website performance.

How We Use Cookies

ModMorrowind Store uses cookies to:

  • Analyze site traffic and usage for performance improvement
  • Provide a personalized shopping experience

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies in your browser settings. Please note, disabling cookies may affect certain site features and functionality, such as keeping you logged in or remembering your cart.
